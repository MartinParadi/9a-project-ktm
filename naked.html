THE DUKE

The DUKE range is proudly naked – not only in design, but also in its raw approach to performance motorcycling. Riding a KTM DUKE is all about pure agile riding fun, wherever you ride, whatever the conditions.

THE EVOLUTION OF FURY

What started life as a 620 cc single-cylinder in 1994, has evolved to include a bloodline of KTM DUKEs – with the KTM 1290 SUPER DUKE R claiming the throne.
