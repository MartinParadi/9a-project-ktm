Enduro

ENDURO / OFFROAD

Enduro is a sport that lies at the heart of KTM. Apart from SuperEnduro, which is held indoors, the word ‘Enduro’ is connected with offroad races that require skill and endurance over varying terrain.

The FIM Hard Enduro World Championship, which has evolved from the WESS Enduro World Championship, features famous races from the extremes of the Red Bull ErzbergRodeo to the grueling Red Bull Romaniacs. The series continues to retain the ethos of WESS under FIM regulation, and 2019 WESS Champion Manuel Lettenbichler will spearhead Red Bull KTM Factory Racing’s campaign in this diverse championship for 2021.

Teammate Josep Garcia, who has enjoyed a number of successful seasons in the WESS series, will contest the 2021 FIM Enduro World Championship in the EnduroGP and Enduro2 classes. These two championships are the perfect platform for KTM to put its Enduro machinery to the ultimate test as the proving ground for development of its READY TO RACE production models. The Red Bull KTM Factory Racing team also competes in the FIM SuperEnduro World Championship, and a number of national enduro events.

In the USA the FMF KTM Factory Racing Team has collected many prestigious offroad accolades over the years. Fielding eight-time GNCC champion Kailub Russell, three-time WORCS champion Taylor Robert, along with 2020 AMA National Extreme Offroad Champion Trystan Hart, two-time GNCC XC2 Pro champion Ben Kelley, 2017 GNCC XC2 champion Josh Toth and AMA National Grand Prix champion Dante Oliveira, it’s an incredible line-up for 2021 in the US offroad series’.
